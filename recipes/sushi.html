Sushi

A japanese food that has become wildly popular in the United States.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Fillings (fish, vegetabiles, etc.)

Steps:

  1. Lay out the seaweed on a flat surface.
  2. Flatten a layer of rice on the seaweed.
  3. Line up contents on top of the seaweed and rice.
  4. Roll up the sushi and cut into 1 inch thick slices.
